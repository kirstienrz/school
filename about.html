History of Probex School

Probex School was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide quality education to students in the community. From its humble beginnings as a small neighborhood school, Probex School has grown into a trusted institution that nurtures students to excel in both academics and personal development.

The school’s founder, JEFF MARVICK DE GULAN, envisioned a place where students would not only learn academic subjects but also be encouraged to explore their talents in various extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, and leadership programs. Today, the school offers a wide range of academic programs for students from kindergarten through high school, with a special focus on cultivating leadership and critical thinking skills.

Founding Story
Probex School's Humble Beginnings

Probex School was founded by JEFF MARVICK DE GULAN, a visionary who believed that education is the key to unlocking the potential of every child. What started as a small community initiative soon blossomed into a full-fledged educational institution, providing students with the tools they need to succeed in both academics and life.

The Timeline of Probex School
Key Milestones
  • 2005: Probex School was founded by JEFF MARVICK DE GULAN with just 20 students.
  • 2010: The school expanded its facilities and added new classrooms and labs.
  • 2015: Probex School launched its Senior High School Program (Grades 11-12).
  • 2020: Introduced advanced teaching methods and technology-based learning tools.
  • 2024: Probex School continues to grow with state-of-the-art facilities and a wide range of academic programs.
